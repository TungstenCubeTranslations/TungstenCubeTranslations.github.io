Chapter 46: Pension Insurance

养老保险是指在职工退休后，由国家或单位按照一定的标准和程序，给予其经济补偿和生活保障的社会保险制度。我国的养老保险制度分为基本养老保险和补充养老保险两个层次。

Basic pension insurance refers to a social insurance system that provides economic compensation and life security to employees after they retire, according to certain standards and procedures, by the state or the employer. China's pension insurance system is divided into two levels: basic pension insurance and supplementary pension insurance.
