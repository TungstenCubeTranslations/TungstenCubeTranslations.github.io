Chapter 156: Noble Freedom

在这个世界上，人们总是在追求着某种自由。有些人追求的是物质上的自由，有些人则是精神上的自由。但是，真正的自由是一种高贵的品质，它不仅仅是简单的追求，更是一种责任和义务。只有在拥有高贵的品质时，才能够真正地享受到自由的快乐。

In this world, people are always pursuing some kind of freedom. Some people pursue material freedom, while others pursue spiritual freedom. However, true freedom is a noble quality. It is not just a simple pursuit, but also a responsibility and obligation. Only when one has noble qualities can they truly enjoy the joy of freedom.
