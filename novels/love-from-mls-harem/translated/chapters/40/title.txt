Chapter 40: Social Public Morality

社会公德是指个人在社会生活中应当遵守的基本道德规范和行为准则。它是社会生活中的一种基本规范，是人们共同遵守的道德规范，是社会文明的重要组成部分。

Social public morality refers to the basic moral norms and behavioral standards that individuals should abide by in social life. It is a fundamental norm in social life, a moral standard that people collectively follow, and an important component of social civilization.
